Indian Head cent

Dates minted: 1859-1909

Mints: Philadelphia and San Francisco

total minted: 1,906,352,400

Designer: James B. Longacre

Description

The Indian Head cent was minted from 1859 to 1909. It has a native American on the obverse and a reef with the words one cent on the reverse.

History

During the civil war the copper was needed for the war so the mint made the pennies thiner to concerve on copper.

Other

The Indian Head penny was the last penny that did not have President Lincoln on it.
