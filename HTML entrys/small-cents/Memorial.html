Memorial cent

Dates minted: 1959-2008

Mints: Philadelphia and Denver

Designer: Obverse Victor D. Brenner, Reverse: Frank Gasparro

Description

The penny has a portrite of President Lincon on the obverse and the Lincoln memorial on the reverse.

History

To celebrate the 150th anervisery of Lincolns birth they change the reverse of the coin from 2 leaves of wheat to the lincoln memorial.
